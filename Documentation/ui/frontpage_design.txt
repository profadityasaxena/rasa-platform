RASA Landing Page — Design Philosophy
======================================

PROMPT INTENT
-------------
The landing page must feel like it was designed by Steve Jobs.
Core values: extreme simplicity, clarity of message, cinematic feeling,
focused call-to-action, beautiful typography, large visual hierarchy.

The visitor should immediately understand:
  "RASA is a new economic system where human contribution creates value."


DESIGN PHILOSOPHY
-----------------
The page follows Apple product storytelling principles:

  1. CHAPTER STRUCTURE
     Each section is a distinct "chapter" — a small label above a large
     headline, followed by minimal supporting text. The visitor reads the
     page as a story, not a feature list.

  2. TYPOGRAPHIC HIERARCHY
     Three levels:
       - Eyebrow label: 11px, letter-spacing 0.45em, uppercase, lotus color
       - Headline: clamp(2rem, vw, max), font-weight 100 (thin), tight tracking
       - Body: 14-18px, font-weight 300 (light), gray (#86868B or #4a4a5a)

     The headline-to-body ratio is intentionally extreme — the headline does
     the storytelling, the body only elaborates for those who want more.

  3. SILENCE AS DESIGN
     White space (or in this case, black space) is a primary design element.
     Section padding is py-28 on mobile, py-40 on desktop.
     Breathing room between elements is generous.

  4. LIGHT IN DARKNESS
     The page uses a deep space black (#050508) as the canvas.
     Soft radial gradient "orbs" provide ambient light:
       - Purple glow (lotus: #C96BCF at ~10% opacity) — top of hero
       - Blue glow (royal: #1E4FA1 at ~7% opacity) — hero right side
       - Center glow in Future section
     These create a cinematic, atmospheric quality without actual images.

  5. ONE ACCENT COLOR
     The lotus color (#C96BCF) is the sole accent — used for:
       - Section eyebrow labels
       - Primary CTA button
       - Decorative rules in How It Works section
       - Wallet card gradient
     All other elements are white, gray, or dark.

  6. SINGLE CALL TO ACTION
     Every CTA is "Join RASA" or "Join the Movement" leading to /register.
     There is no noise — one destination.


COLOR PALETTE
-------------
Background (deep):   #050508  — hero, idea, wallet, CTA
Background (raised): #070710  — problem, organizations sections
Background (darkest):#030305  — future section (most dramatic)
Background (mid):    #080810  — how it works section

Text primary:        #F5F5F7  — Apple's near-white (not pure white, softer)
Text secondary:      #86868B  — Apple's secondary gray
Text tertiary:       #4a4a5a  — supporting copy, low contrast on purpose
Text ghost:          #252530  — barely-visible elements (numbers, borders)

Accent — Lotus:      #C96BCF  — primary brand accent
Accent — Mint:       #5ED3A5  — secondary (wallet, how-it-works step 2)
Accent — Royal:      #1E4FA1  — tertiary (wallet glow, how-it-works step 3)
Accent — Sun:        #FFD60A  — used only in wallet memory tokens


TYPOGRAPHY
----------
Font stack: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif
  → Renders as SF Pro on Apple devices (iOS/macOS)
  → Renders as Segoe UI on Windows
  → Clean, modern, no web font loading delay

Hero headline:      clamp(2.8rem, 6.5vw, 7.5rem), weight 100, tracking -0.025em
Section headlines:  clamp(2rem, 4.5vw, 4.5rem), weight 100, tracking -0.02em
Sub-headlines:      clamp(1.1rem, 2vw, 1.35rem), weight 300
Body copy:          14-18px, weight 300
Labels/eyebrows:    11px, weight 500, tracking 0.45em, uppercase
Card numbers:       60px, weight 100 (purely decorative, very low opacity)


USER JOURNEY
------------
1. Hero        — First impression. Understand RASA in 10 seconds.
2. Problem     — "I understand why this exists."
3. Idea        — "This is the solution I didn't know existed."
4. How Works   — "I understand how to participate."
5. Wallet      — "I understand what I earn."
6. Organizations — "I understand who uses this."
7. Future      — "I believe in the vision."
8. CTA         — "I'm ready to join."

Each section advances emotional commitment, not just information.
The visitor who reaches the CTA has already decided — the button just confirms it.
